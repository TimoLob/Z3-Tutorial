\begin{frame}{Problem 1 - Formalization}
This can be expressed as a linear programming problem.  
\begin{align}
\max f(x) = 10 * A + 30 * B + 50 * C \\
\text{with contraints}\\
3*A + 5*B+ 4*C <= 500 \\
3*A + 3*B + 5*C <= 800 \\
4*A + 2*B + 6*C <= 600 \\
A>=0 \\
B>=0 \\
C>=0
\end{align}
\end{frame}
